ABOUT PRIME STOP

Our Story

Prime Stop was founded in 1989 as it was originally known, Wisner West by owners Herb and Kathy Albers. In 1997, a second location opened in Wayne, Nebraska. The Norfolk, Nebraska location was added in 2003. Also in 2003, the three locations became part of the Shell brand and the names changed to Prime Stop. All three locations are open 24/7. Prime Stops pride themselves on clean stores, courteous, prompt, and friendly customer service!

OUR CORE VALUES

We value the principals of responsible, honest employees who foster teamwork and display positivity. Our goal as a profitable business is to reflect the principals of honesty, work ethic, kindness, and loyalty. Adhering to the qualities of self-control in opinions, speech, and habits creates the positive work environment key to success.
